The African Wireless Communications Yearbook is the must-have guide for all vendors and suppliers who want to capitalise on the continent’s booming wireless and mobile communications markets.

Now in its eight year, the 2015 African Wireless Communications Yearbook is packed with essential information needed for commercial success. The African Wireless Communications Yearbook features chapters on the technologies that are bringing voice, data and broadband connectivity across the region. The African Wireless Communications Yearbook is the ultimate reference tool and gives you actionable business insights to help you flourish in this booming wireless communications market.
