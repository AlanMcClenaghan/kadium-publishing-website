The African Wireless Communications Yearbook 2010 offers an invaluable guide to wireless communications technology throughout Africa in 2010 and offers expert analysis and vital statistics illustrating usage and market trends in cellular, satcoms, WiFi, WiMax, P2P and mission critical technologies such as PMR and TETRA.

The 2010 edition focuses on how these wireless networks are being built, run and how revenues and performance are being maximised. It also rounds up the latest developments in African regulations and includes a comprehensive Buyer's Guide to complete the picture.

This Buyers Guide forms a crucial part of the Yearbook and provides readers with an at-a-glance reference tool to all the key suppliers in Africa's communications marketplace.
