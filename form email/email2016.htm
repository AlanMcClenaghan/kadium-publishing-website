As Africa continue's to be the world's fastest growing region for wireless and mobile communications, specialised insight and data is essential for all vendors and suppliers aiming to succeed on the continent.

The African Wireless Communications Yearbook is the ultimate reference tool, and delivers the crucial business information needed to flourish in this booming market.

Now in its ninth year, the 2016 African Wireless Communications Yearbook gives readers a comprehensive profile of the region's business hotspots. It focuses on the technology and financial trends that are shaping the continent's markets, and includes exclusive comments from expert vendors, service providers, suppliers and industry insiders.

Each edition provides a detailed overview of the continent's current investment climate, as well as a 'health report' of its cellular, satellite, fixed wireless, and critical communications markets. There are also special focus areas covering value-added services, broadband, backhaul and green technologies.
